Who Uses This System?

  • Suppliers - Register medicines and manage deliveries
  • Facilities (Pharmacies) - Request supplies and monitor stock
  • Inspectors - Schedule and record pharmacy inspections
  • Administrators - Review applications, issue licenses and generate reports
