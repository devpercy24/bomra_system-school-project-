DROP YOUR BACKGROUND IMAGES HERE
=================================
Place your 4 images in this folder with these exact filenames:

  bg1.jpg  — batch 1, left panel
  bg2.jpg  — batch 1, right panel
  bg3.jpg  — batch 2, left panel
  bg4.jpg  — batch 2, right panel

Any format works (jpg, png, webp) — just update the filenames
in frontend/css/bomra.css under the BACKGROUND IMAGE SLIDESHOW
section if you use different names.

The images will be blurred and dimmed automatically by the CSS.
