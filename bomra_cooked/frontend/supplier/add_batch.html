Add a Medicine Batch

Register a new medicine batch. After adding a batch, submit a registration application for BoMRA review before it can be distributed.

Workflow: Add batch → Submit application → Admin reviews → Approved batch available for delivery.

Include the strength and form (e.g. Amoxicillin 250mg Capsules).
Full legal name of the manufacturing company.
As printed on the medicine packaging — must be unique per manufacturer.
The expiry date printed on the batch packaging.
